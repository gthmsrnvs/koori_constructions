About Us

Koori Constructions is a Central Coast based carpentry business servicing all over the Central Coast, Lake Macquarie and Newcastle areas. We are proudly 100% Indigenous owned and cover all aspects of carpentry. The owner of the business, Brad Robinson, is a proud Dunghutti and Wiradjuri man who has lived on Darkinjung country (Central Coast) his whole life and started this business to try and give back to his community any way he can.

We offer a wide range of construction services including:
  • Decks & Pergolas
  • Bathrooms
  • Kitchens
  • Cladding
  • Internal & External Fixouts
  • Fit offs
